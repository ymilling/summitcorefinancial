Student-to-Student Financial Guidance

Building your financial foundation, one step at a time

Hello! I'm Yoon, a rising senior double majoring in Economics and Computer Science with a Math minor at Penn State. I know firsthand how overwhelming finances can feel while allocating time for difficult classes, internships, and (potentially) a social life. That's why I created Summit Core Financial - to help fellow undergrads develop smart money habits that will last a lifetime, no matter what school you attend.

Why Learn From a Peer?

I get it - I'm living it too. From stretching dining dollars to deciding whether that unpaid internship is worth it, I understand the real financial decisions college students face. I've spent the last two years diving deep into personal finance, not just through my economics courses but through books, certifications, and real-world practice. Now I want to share what I've learned in a way that actually makes sense for our generation.

My Promise

No confusing jargon. No judgment about your current situation. No pushing products you don't need. Just practical, actionable education from someone who understands exactly where you're coming from.

What I Offer

Smart Budgeting

Learn how to create a realistic budget that works with your student lifestyle. I'll teach you tracking methods, money-saving strategies, and how to balance saving with actually enjoying college.

Student Loan Management

Navigate student loans confidently. Understand your options, minimize debt, and create a realistic repayment strategy.

Investing 101

Start investing with as little as $10. Learn about index funds, compound interest, and building long-term wealth.

Career Prep

Learn the fundamentals of workplace benefits like 401(k)s and IRAs to make informed decisions when you start your career.

Why Summit Core Financial?

As a fellow undergrad, I've been overwhelmed in the past. It was difficult to start my journey to understanding the fundamentals of personal finance especially in this day and age where there's so much garbage at the tip of our fingerprints: YouTubers guaranteeing short term growth through memecoins, an ever increasing amount of online courses, or brokerages that lets you put your savings into guessing the winner of a hotdog eating contest.

I am here to simplify the essentials of personal finance for undergrads before a commission-based financial advisor promotes a fund that gives a kickback that also underperforms the market. I believe that a 30 minute consultation ALONE can set any amount of students up to understand what the immediate steps to be taken are. Through Summit Core Financial, we can guarantee a dramatic increase in being weary and calculated in a world of confusion.

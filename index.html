    Why Summit Core Financial?

    As a fellow undergrad, I've been overwhelmed in the past. It was difficult to start my journey to understanding the fundamentals of personal finance especially in this day and age where there's so much garbage at the tip of our fingerprints: YouTubers guaranteeing short term growth through memecoins, an ever increasing amount of online courses, or brokerages that lets you put your savings into guessing the winner of a hotdog eating contest.

    I am here to simplify the essentials of personal finance for undergrads before a commission-based financial advisor promotes a fund that gives a kickback that also underperforms the market. I believe that a 30 minute consultation ALONE can set any amount of students up to understand what the immediate steps to be taken are. Through Summit Core Financial, we can guarantee a dramatic increase in being weary and calculated in a world of confusion.
